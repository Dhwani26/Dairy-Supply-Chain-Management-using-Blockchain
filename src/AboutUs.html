Our Vision

  1. To become leaders in the Indian Dairy Industry, while retaining our Indian traditions and family values.
  2. Be the first choice for consumers.
  3. Give our customers value for money. This will be through improvement in technologies used in our Dairy, thus enhancing the quality of our raw material, our internal processes, and the competency of our people.
  4. Provide our customers with the cleanest and healthiest milk, thus leading to more nutritional milk and milk products.
  5. Be a preferred employer in our field, and provide an environment that challenges our employees to learn, grow and prosper in an atmosphere of respect and recognition.

Our Mission

  1. Inspire sustained growth for our entire workforce and farmers who have engaged in long term partnerships with us
  2. Understanding customer needs and protecting their interests to build lifelong relationships and brand loyalty

Quality & Food Safety Objectives

  1. To increase customer satisfaction by reducing the instances of pilferages in Liquid Milk pouches.
  2. To modify & enhance processes as per customer expectation and market trends at least one per year for improving Quality & Safety of product.
